// ABOUT ME

Who I Am

I'm Jeleel, a data analyst based in Abuja, Nigeria. I hold a B.Tech in Industrial Chemistry from Federal University of Technology Minna and completed my NYSC service year at the National Identity Management Commission (NIMC), Abuja, where I supported procurement data processes and financial bid documentation.

My journey into data evolved through the ALX Africa Data Analytics programme, where I built deep expertise in SQL, Power BI, and data storytelling — working on real-world datasets spanning water access, government procurement, and global cinema.

I believe data is most powerful when it drives real decisions — not just reports. Every project in my portfolio is built around that principle.

Currently expanding into Python and working toward Data Engineering as my next career milestone.
